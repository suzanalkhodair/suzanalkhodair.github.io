Reaching the publication phase in your research is a great accomplishment, and your hard work deserves to be given maximum care at this stage as you prepare to share it with the world. I am a board-certified editor in the life sciences with over 10 years’ experience, and I’m here to help! My aim is to ensure that your manuscript is clear, concise, written in a professional academic tone, and free from structural issues. Originally a physician, I have been proudly helping medical professionals, researchers, and higher education candidates prepare their work for publication. My skills are especially helpful to authors writing in English as a second language. Being a native speaker of both English and Arabic, I often help writers formulate complex ideas and pick up incorrect use of expressions or phrases that would have an impact on the intended meaning. I’ve learned a great deal from every person I have worked with and have grown wiser because of them. In my eleventh year as a freelancer, I look forward to collaborating with many others and hearing the news of their success.

Editing

Over the years I have edited and proofread material in medicine, surgery, nursing, pharmacy, physical therapy, nutrition, psychology, psychiatry, special education, medical education, and epidemiology, to name a few. Among my many achievements, I have worked on:

  • Research articles, reviews, case reports
  • Replying to reviewer comments
  • Theses for local and international students
  • Webpage content for health associations
  • Practice manuals
  • Books
  • Personal statements
  • Research proposals
  • Presentations and posters

Translation

I am also an experienced translator between Arabic and English in the medical field with over 10 years of experience. I started out translating patient handout material, then went on to translate webpage content, psychological scales, practice manuals, and books. I’ve also donated over 40,000 words to Translator Without Borders, who provide services to organization such as the WHO, the UN, and the Red Cross. Among my accomplishments are:

  • Reviewing a COVID-19 glossary
  • Reviewing full book translations
  • Theses
  • Full journal articles
  • Complete material for a psychology class
  • Audio scripts on psychological wellness
  • Webpages for organizations
  • Participant interviews
  • Questionnaires, psychological scales
  • Health SMS messages during the pandemic
