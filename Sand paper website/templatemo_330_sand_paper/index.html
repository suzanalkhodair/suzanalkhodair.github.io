You dedicated a lot of hard work to your research. It deserves to be presented in the best way possible. I'm a physician and a board-certified medical editor with over 10 years' experience, and I'm here to help! While not all journals require you to have your manuscript edited professionally before submitting, it is important to deal with language issues and ensure your ideas are presented clearly and concisely and in a professional academic tone. It is also imperative to use inclusive and appropriate language, especially with cultural expressions or when discussing sensitive issues.

Whether you are writing an article, case report, thesis, or practice guide, I can make sure that your manuscript follows the correct format.

If you don't find the service you need in the lists below, get in touch and let's discuss how I can help you.

Editing Services

  • Expert medical manuscript editing and proofreading
  • Medical writing assistance
  • Manuscript formating following journal instructions
  • Reference checking and formatting (Vancouver, APA, ACS, Harvard, etc.)
  • Replying to reviewer comments
  • Thesis editing and formating following APA style and university guidelines

Translation Services

  • Expert translation to and from Arabic in any health-related field
  • Forward and backward translation of research tools
  • Patient handouts; webpage content; health promotional material
  • YouTube video captioning in English and Arabic
  • Book translation
